Putting emphasis on quality assurance and obeying laws is the tide all over the world. Textile industry is absolutely one of them. Nissenken guarantees textile quality and obeys relevant rules at each stage of processing from manufacturing to retailing. Especially, as the only one Oeko-Tex research and test institute in Japan, Nissenken has professional technology of determining harmful substances. Textiles with Oeko-Tex certification are highly recognized in many countries. Besides, Nissenken also involved in the testing of leather, accessories, daily necessities, toys and other stuff by using knowledge acquired in testing practice. If you have any question, please contact us. All the testing methods are carried out based on practical standards.

The laboratory covers a whole gamut of textile testing from testing of fibres to fabrics for Color Fastness tests, Physical tests, Endurance tests, Functional and Analysis tests as follows;

  • Color Fastness to light, perspiration, rubbing, washing, dry cleaning, chlorinated water, etc;
  • Physical tests like tensile strength and elongation, tearing strength, bursting strength, abrasion resistance, seam slippage resistance, fabric analysis, yarn count, etc;
  • Dimensional change tests like resistance to laundering, repeated washing, dry cleaning, heat pressing and steaming, etc;
  • Functional Tests like water repellency, water absorbency and quick drying property, etc;
  • Analytical tests to detect and quantify harmful substances like Formaldehyde and Banned Amines (Azo dyes) in textiles using state of the art machines.
