Putting emphasis on quality assurance and obeying laws is the tide all over the world. Textile industry is absolutely one of them. Nissenken guarantees textile quality and obeys relevant rules at each stage of processing from manufacturing to retailing. Especially, as the only one Oeko-Tex research and test institute in Japan, Nissenken has professional technology of determining harmful substances. Textiles with Oeko-Tex certification are highly recognized in many countries. Besides, Nissenken also involved in the testing of leather, accessories, daily necessities, toys and other stuff by using knowledge acquired in testing practice. If you have any question, please contact us. All the testing methods are carried out based on practical standards.
