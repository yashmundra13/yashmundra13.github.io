About Nissenken

Nissenken was established in 1948 as a Testing and Research Institute in the field of textiles.

Since then, it has grown rapidly and is now a multinational company with its head office located in Tokyo and 12 branches providing testing and inspection services in Japan, 10 in China, 2 in Indonesia, 2 in Myanmar and 1 in Bangladesh and Cambodia each. Nissenken has been pioneering the research and development work and serving the global textile industry.

Nissenken offers unbiased and accurate testing and inspection services to the textile industry covering a whole gamut of textile testing right from testing of fibres to fabrics and finished garments. Besides, we also engage in socially contributing to make a comfortable and healthy living environment by assessing new fiber materials catering to market demands. After becoming one member of Oeko-Tex international safety certification institutes in 2000, Nissenken, with fairness and globalization opinion, as the only one recognized certification institution of Oeko-Tex, set our mission as the safety bond combining consumers, products and manufacturers together
